# chemmaster-quiz
Short quiz for Secondary School Students taking O-level Chemistry 6092 in Singapore

This is a mini browser project by me to aid secondary school students in their O-level Chemistry revision. 

It is a gamified quiz platform and covers the topics of Experimental Chemistry, Kinetic Particle Theory, Chemical Bonding & Structure, Molar Calculations, Acid-Base Chemistry, Redox reactions, Rate of Reaction, Chemical Energetics, and Periodic Table.

To try out the quiz, download ChemMaster-quiz.html and run it on your own device.

It may be buggy and have some errors, but please enjoy!
